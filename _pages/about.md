I am a postdoctoral researcher in the [MIND](https://team.inria.fr/mind/) team, *Inria Saclay*, working with [Thomas Moreau](https://tommoral.github.io/). 

Prior to this, I was a PhD student in Statistics at [LAMA](https://lama.u-pem.fr/), *Université Gustave Eiffel* and [LPSM](https://www.lpsm.paris/), *Sorbonne Université*, under the supervision of [Christophe Denis](https://www.pantheonsorbonne.fr/page-perso/christophe.denis1%40) (LAMA, LPSM), [Charlotte Dion-Blanc](https://sites.google.com/site/charlottedionblanc/) (LPSM), [Laure Sansonnet](https://mia-ps.inrae.fr/laure-sansonnet) (MIA Paris-Saclay) and [Yves Bas](https://cesco.mnhn.fr/fr/annuaire/yves-bas-6041) (CESCO, PatriNat). \
My thesis was funded by a grant from *Ecole Polytechnique*, through the chair [Modélisation Mathématique et Biodiversité (MMB)](http://www.cmap.polytechnique.fr/chaire-mmb/index.html).
